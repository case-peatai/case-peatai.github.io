bio: |
  Nengbo Wang is a PhD student in Computer Science and Marketing (dual field) at Case Western Reserve University. His research focuses on Large Language Models (LLMs), Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), AI Agents, and Causality, bridging the gap between cutting-edge AI techniques and real-world applications.

  Before returning to academia, Nengbo spent two years as an Applied Scientist at Yum R&D Center (KFC, Pizza Hut, Taco Bell), where he independently designed and deployed AI/ML systems across 10,000+ stores, impacting over 10 million users. He also served as a Data Scientist at MetersBonwe Group, building ML algorithms for 1,000+ retailers.

  His academic work has been published at top-tier venues including ACL 2025 and the Journal of Personal Selling and Sales Management. He holds 7 patents in AI and has received the Excellent Researcher Student Award (top-20 university-wide) at CWRU. He serves as an instructor for Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence and Marketing Analytics (MKM 2310).
