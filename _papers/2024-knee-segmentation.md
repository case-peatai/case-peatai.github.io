abstract: "This study proposes a novel unsupervised method for the fully automated segmentation of Bone Marrow Edema-like Lesions (BMEL) in knee MRI. By leveraging conditional diffusion models and anomaly detection, the approach eliminates the need for labor-intensive and bias-prone manual annotations. The research sets new benchmarks for BMEL segmentation performance and provides a more reliable, quantitative tool for early diagnosis and prognosis of knee osteoarthritis."
