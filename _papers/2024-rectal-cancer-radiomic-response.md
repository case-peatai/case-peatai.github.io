abstract: |
  This study analyzes intra-tumoral and peri-tumoral radiomic features from
  pretreatment MRI to predict pathologic response in rectal cancer. It evaluates
  whether quantitative imaging phenotypes can identify responders across
  multiple neoadjuvant treatment regimens. The work aims to improve patient
  stratification beyond traditional staging and biomarker approaches. It
  contributes to noninvasive response prediction in rectal cancer management.
