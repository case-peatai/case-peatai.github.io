abstract: |
  This paper studies evaluation metrics for test-time scaling by separating
  answer discovery from repeated correctness. It derives Geom@k and the broader
  GeoSpectrum@K family from a common hypergeometric view of fixed-budget
  metrics. Across aggregate settings, Geom@2 provides a strong balance of fast
  convergence and low ranking drift relative to alternative summaries. The work
  offers a compute-aware perspective on stable evaluation under repeated
  sampling.
