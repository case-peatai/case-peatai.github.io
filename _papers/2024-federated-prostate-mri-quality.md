abstract: |
  This work addresses image-quality variability in prostate MRI across multiple
  institutions using a federated analysis setting. It studies how artifact-
  related quality differences can affect the reliability and portability of
  downstream machine learning models. The abstract highlights the importance of
  multi-institutional quality assessment before model development and
  deployment. It contributes to more reliable imaging AI in federated and
  heterogeneous clinical environments.
