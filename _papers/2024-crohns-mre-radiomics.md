abstract: |
  This study develops radiomics-based machine learning models to characterize
  inflammation and fibrosis in Crohn's disease strictures from magnetic
  resonance enterography. The models improve diagnostic discrimination relative
  to radiologist visual scoring alone and show additional value when combined
  with expert assessment. The work addresses an important unmet need in
  noninvasive characterization of stricturing disease. It supports more
  quantitative and reproducible imaging-based assessment in inflammatory bowel
  disease.
