abstract: |
  This work-in-progress explores how AI can improve the efficiency of creating
  multilingual auditory test materials, with a focus on Spanish BEL sentences.
  The project investigates workflow acceleration and quality support for
  multilingual assessment design. It sits at the intersection of language
  technology, hearing research, and educational test development. The aim is to
  reduce manual burden while preserving the validity of test materials.
