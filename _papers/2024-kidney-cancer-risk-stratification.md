abstract: |
  This abstract reports a deep learning model that uses pre-operative CT scans
  to predict overall survival in kidney cancer. The model improves pre-operative
  risk assessment and offers prognostic value beyond standard
  clinicopathological factors. It represents an early step toward multi-
  institutional imaging biomarkers for survival-based treatment planning. The
  work supports broader use of CT-derived representations for oncologic
  prognostication.
